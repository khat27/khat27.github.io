Kompas.id Redesign

I made this design in July as a requirement for applying UI Designer position at Kompas.id internship program. After analyzing how their application works and understanding their design "language", I started making this design by considering what are the things that can be improved in their design. and finally, I came up with this. They might not use this now, but you might be familiar with Line Today's Interface as they now use the design that look very similar to what I made for Kompas.id.
